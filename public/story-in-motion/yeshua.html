The Story Behind the Story

Meet Yeshua

Yeshua sees the world in a way others don't always understand. His gift isn't loud — it's quiet, creative, and deeply purposeful. Through his journey, children discover that what makes them different is exactly what makes them powerful.

This cinematic experience is designed to take everything that lives in the pages of the book and bring it into a world of motion, music, and emotional depth — honoring the story while expanding its reach.
