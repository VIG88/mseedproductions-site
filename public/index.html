Who We Are

A New Kind of
Children's Studio

MustardSeed Productions is the children's storytelling and television production arm of Visionaires Media — creating emotionally rich, imaginative, and purpose-driven content for the next generation.

We develop books, animated storytelling, and television properties designed to inspire, elevate, and leave a lasting imprint on every child who encounters them.

Our mission is to plant seeds through story — seeds of courage, growth, imagination, and purpose — so young audiences can believe in who they are becoming.

"From small seeds, powerful stories grow."
