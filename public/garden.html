Who We Are

A New Kind of
Children's Studio

MustardSeed Productions is the children's storytelling and television production arm of Visionaires Media — dedicated to creating meaningful, imaginative, and emotionally rich content for the next generation.

We develop children's books, animated storytelling, and television properties designed to entertain, inspire, and leave a lasting imprint.

Our mission is to plant seeds through story — seeds of courage, growth, imagination, and purpose — so young audiences can believe in who they are becoming.

"From small seeds, powerful stories grow."
