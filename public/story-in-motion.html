Story in Motion

More Than Animation

Story in Motion is the animated extension of the Seeds of Greatness book series — cinematic short films created to immerse children in the worlds, emotions, and journeys of Yeshua, Messiah, Levi, and Jadery.

Each Story in Motion experience pairs original visuals with intentional sound design, bringing the emotional depth of the books into a new dimension. These are not cartoons. These are cinematic experiences created with the care and intention of films that parents remember and children carry into adulthood.

We are in active development. Each character's story will receive its own dedicated animated experience — crafted to honor the original book and expand its world with new depth and beauty.

What to Expect

The Vision

Pillar One

Cinematic Visual Language

Each animated story will carry the same premium visual tone as the books — gold-lit environments, warm character palettes, and an aesthetic language built for emotional resonance, not just entertainment.

Pillar Two

Intentional Sound Design

Original music and ambient sound will be composed to match each character's emotional world — from Messiah's rhythm-driven energy to Jadery's bilingual warmth and Yeshua's quiet creative depth.

Pillar Three

Designed for Families

These experiences are made to be watched together — stories where parents recognize the themes and children feel seen. Every frame is crafted with the intention that it will be remembered long after the screen is off.

Pillar Four

Faithful to the Books

Every animated adaptation begins with deep fidelity to the source material — the character voice, the emotional truth, and the message that lives at the heart of each story. We expand the world without diluting the seed.
