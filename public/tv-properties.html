  • Short-form and long-form animated content

  • Seeds of Greatness animated adaptations
  • Original animated IP development

Television Productions

Beyond animation, MustardSeed Productions develops live-action and hybrid television properties that expand our narrative universe across episodic formats. We build worlds that reward long-term viewership — stories with arcs that deepen over seasons, characters that grow alongside their audiences.

Our approach to television is deliberate and premium: fewer projects, executed with greater depth and intention.

  • Long-form episodic series development
  • Faith-grounded family drama and adventure
  • Multi-season narrative architecture
  • Hybrid live-action and animated formats
  • Platform partnership and licensing strategy
  • Audience development for the next generation

Our Approach

Quality Without Compromise

We believe children's content has been consistently underestimated — treated as easy to produce and quick to consume. We reject that model entirely.

Every production we develop is held to a standard of craft that respects the intelligence, emotional depth, and spiritual hunger of the children and families we serve. Great children's content is not simple content — it is content that meets its audience where they are and takes them somewhere they have never been.

That is the standard we hold ourselves to. Always.
