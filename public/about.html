Our Mission

Stories That Root. Worlds That Grow.

MustardSeed Productions exists to create stories that matter — narratives rooted in truth, shaped by purpose, and built to grow with the children and families who experience them.

We believe that the most powerful stories are not the ones that entertain alone, but the ones that quietly plant something deep — a sense of identity, a courage to face challenges, a vision of who a child is called to become.

From the very first frame to the final page, every project we develop is guided by one question: Does this story plant a seed that will grow into something greater?

What We Create

Four Pillars of Storytelling

Our creative output spans four interconnected disciplines, each designed to reach children and families at different moments of their lives.

📖

Children's Books

Beautifully crafted picture books and chapter books that introduce young readers to characters who face real-life challenges with faith, resilience, and heart.

🎬

Animated Storytelling

Premium animated series and shorts that bring our characters to life — rich visual worlds with emotional depth, designed for the streaming age.

📺

Television Productions

Long-form television projects that build worlds viewers can return to — episodic storytelling that grows alongside its audience across seasons and years.

🌱

Next-Generation IP

Franchise-ready intellectual property built for the long arc — characters and worlds designed to expand across media, merchandise, and cultural conversation.

Parent Company

A Visionaires Media Company

MustardSeed Productions is the children's storytelling and television production arm of Visionaires Media — a premium media holding company committed to building culturally significant, purpose-driven intellectual property.

Visionaires Media provides the infrastructure, investment framework, and strategic vision that allows MustardSeed Productions to operate with the creative freedom of an independent studio and the resources of a larger media enterprise.

Together, we are building a legacy — not just a catalogue of content, but a body of work that will be passed down, remembered, and returned to for generations.
